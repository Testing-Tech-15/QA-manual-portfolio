# BR-001: App Crashes When Tapping Ellipsis Menu on Scheduled Item Card

---

## General Information
- **Assigned To:** QA Engineer  
- **Date:** 02 January 2026  
- **Priority:** High  
- **Status:** Not Started  
- **Reproducibility:** 100%  
- **Attachments:** Video / Screenshot (if available)  

---

## Environment
- **Device:** Galaxy S21 Ultra 5G (Test Device)  
- **OS:** Android 13  
- **App Version:** One UI 5.1  

---

## Precondition
- User is logged in to the app  
- At least one Scheduled Item is available  
- The card displays an ellipsis menu (⋮)  

---

## Steps to Reproduce
1. Open the application  
2. Navigate to the **Items** section  
3. Select any Scheduled Item card  
4. Tap the ellipsis menu (⋮) at the top-right corner of the card  

---

## Actual Result
- App immediately crashes  
- White screen appears and app becomes unresponsive  

---

## Expected Result
- Ellipsis menu should open without delay  
- All associated actions should be visible and functional  

---

## Test Data (Anonymized)
- User: Authorized test user (NDA-protected)  
- Scheduled Item: Any card with ellipsis menu
